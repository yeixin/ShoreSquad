About ShoreSquad

Our Mission

ShoreSquad mobilizes young people to clean beaches and protect our oceans through coordinated, social, and fun cleanup events. We combine real-time weather tracking, interactive maps, and community features to make environmental action accessible and engaging.

Why We Started

Our coasts face unprecedented plastic pollution and debris. Traditional cleanup efforts lack coordination and community engagement. ShoreSquad changes that by making beach cleanups easy to organize, find, and join‚Äîturning eco-action into a social movement.

Our Values

  • Impact: Every cleanup removes real pollution from our beaches
  • Community: We believe collective action is more powerful than individual effort
  • Accessibility: Cleanup events should be easy to discover and join for anyone
  • Transparency: We're open about our data and how we operate

Built For You

ShoreSquad is a lightweight, privacy-first web app built with vanilla JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. No bloatware, no unnecessary tracking‚Äîjust a clean, fast app to coordinate beach cleanups.

Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

ShoreSquad collects minimal data:

  • Location: Only when you click "Find Nearby" and share it with us
  • Events: Events you create and join are stored locally in your browser

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
